Hotel Shobla Royale (Kullu) is located in the heart of the city right next to the famous Dussehra ground. It boasts of a total of 36 rooms divided into Deluxe, Executive and Suite Rooms with an In-house Multiplex, Multi-Cuisine Restaurant, Matala - The Bar, Domino’s Pizza outlet, Elevator, Games Room and Ample Parking space within the complex. All rooms are newly renovated and refurbished with all modern amenities. An ideal stopover for solo travellers, couples, friends and families looking to explore the Kullu valley. Kullu is known as the "Valley Of Gods" or " Dev Bhoomi " due to its deeply rooted rich traditions and cultural values. Shobla Kaathkuni Royale (Kullu) is a wooden masterpiece which seamlessly blends local architecture with the modern amenities and furnishings. ‘Kaathkuni’ is a term given to the local architectural design which involves interlocking of wood and stones. The mud plastered walls further enhances the room ambiance as well as keeps the room naturally insulated. The detailed and intricate carving on wood is a delight to watch in this modern era and shall only remind you of the good old days. The cottages has valley facing rooms with private balcony on the first floor and the ground floor rooms are attached to the garden area.
Shobla Pine Royale (Manali) is a luxurious retreat nestled in Old Manali (5 kms from main the mall road market and 2 kms from Old Manali market). We offer a boutique stay overlooking the mighty river Beas. Luxurious and spacious wooden panelled rooms come with a private balcony and other modern amenities ensuring a comfortable stay for all. Panoramic view of the snow-capped mountains and waterfall from every room will leave you mesmerized. One can walk around the apple orchards or walk down to the river bed and unwind in the lap of nature. Shobla Pine Royale is an ideal pick for spending a peaceful vacation and having a quality time in the Himalayas away from the hustle and bustle of the city life.
